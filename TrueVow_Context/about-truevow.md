## What TrueVow is
TrueVow is an AI-native SaaS platform for **personal-injury (PI) law firms**. It runs the pre-litigation intelligence pipeline end to end so a solo attorney — no paralegal, no IT support — can do work that used to take a team.

## The product pipeline — Capture → Build → Protect
- **INTAKE** *(Capture)* — AI voice intake captures the injured caller, qualifies and grades the lead, routes it to the firm.
- **TRACE** *(Build)* — after a retainer is signed, automates medical-record retrieval and builds a demand-ready, source-cited treatment chronology.
- **SETTLE** *(Protect)* — an ethical, attorney-owned settlement database giving data-backed settlement ranges to counter insurer lowballs.

## Supporting tenant products
- **LEVERAGE** (ex-DRAFT) — zero-knowledge document compliance validation across all 50 states.
- **VERIFY** — blockchain-anchored (OpenTimestamps/Bitcoin) certificates for legal admissibility.
- **Customer Portal** — the attorney's single dashboard across all products.

## Platform / internal
- **SaaS Admin** — tenant management + the **MDM system of record** (mints the canonical `case_id`/`contact_id`).
- **Billing** — subscriptions, invoicing, payments. **Sales Ops** — acquire firms. **Customer Success** — retain them.
- **Internal Ops** + **Financial Management** — run the company. **Platform Analytics** — measure the pipeline.

## Who we serve (never forget)
The end user is a **frightened injured person on a phone** and the **solo PI attorney** who stakes their reputation on our output in front of adjusters, judges, and their own client. Every decision serves them — see `voice-and-standards.md`.
